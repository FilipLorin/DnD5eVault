### Druid Circle
At 2nd level, you choose to identify with a circle of druids. Your choice grants you features at 2nd level and again at 6th, 10th, and 14th level.
- [[Circle of Dreams]]
- [[Circle of the Land]]
- [[Circle of the Moon]]
- [[Shepherd]]
- [[Circle of Spores]]
- [[Circle of Stars]]
- [[Wildfire]]
